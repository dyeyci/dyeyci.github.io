haikyu!!

Haikyu!! is a shonen sports anime series based on Haruichi Furudate's manga series of the same name. It was developed by Production I.G and Toho in cooperation with the Japanese television network MBS. The anime is divided into four seasons, four feature films, and five OVAs. Sentai Filmworks licensed the anime in the United States and dubbed seasons one through three in English.

Shoyo Hinata, a junior high school student, becomes obsessed with volleyball after catching a glimpse of Karasuno High School playing in the Nationals on TV. Of short stature himself, Hinata is inspired by a player the commentators nickname 'The Little Giant', Karasuno's short but talented wing-spiker. He joins his school's volleyball club, only to find he is its sole member. Hinata and third-year Tobio Kageyama, a prodigy setter nicknamed 'The King of the Court', sparked a short rivalry after losing their first official game together. The duo create an explosive new volleyball tactic and develop an unexpected but powerful setter-spikers partnership. Along the way, they push each other into reaching their full potential and Hinata develops relationships with his first real team, thus beginning Karasuno's journey of redemption to restore their reputation and make it to the Nationals.
